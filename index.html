Mechanical Engineer

My name is Solomon Andrews. I am a recent Mechanical Engineering graduate from the FAMU-FSU College of Engineering, with a journey marked by a passion for innovation and a track record of success, including awards, certifications, and impactful research experiences.

Throughout my academic career, I have honed my skills in additive manufacturing, electronics, programming, propulsion systems, material science, biotech, and mechanical design. Internships and research roles at esteemed institutions like Honeywell KCNSC, NASA MSFC, Mayo Clinic, FAMU-FSU HPMI, and FAMU-FSU AME have enriched my practical insights and skills. I am eager to apply my knowledge and creativity to solve real-world challenges.
